How to Register

Are you a Strategic Communication student at the Missouri School of Journalism? Are you interested in Event Planning and Promotions? Follow the steps below to join our team and be a part of PRI-MO's next event teams!

  • Visit MU Strat Comm page to learn more about prerequisites for this course.
  • Email Melanie Forrest to receive a permission number.
  • Enroll in J4672 on MyZou.

Meet the PRI-MO Team

All events by PRI-MO are planned, organized, designed, and executed by students in the Events and Promotions course using the Missouri Method through the Missouri School of Journalism. Students can choose to work in one of four roles, account, strategy, design, and entertainment.
